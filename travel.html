Travel to Saints Field

The best place to catch a baseball game in Central Minnesota is hands down, Saints Field in Dassel. Catch a game from the newly renovated third base deck while enjoying a beer from Pauly the Beer Man or enjoy a hot dog from Dawn Lindquist at the concession stand.

Things to do
  • Watch the Saints play baseball
  • Enjoy a beer on the Deck with Saints Super Fans
  • Play "Hot Box" with the local youth
